Last-Minute Preparation Tips

Download your boarding passes
Most airlines allow online check-in 24 hours before departure. Download your boarding passes to avoid queues at the airport.
Charge your devices
Make sure your phone, tablet, camera, and other devices are fully charged before departure.
Check your documents
Ensure your passport, ID, travel insurance, and other important documents are packed and easily accessible.
Download offline maps
Save your destination map for offline use in case you don't have internet access upon arrival.
Save your trip for offline access
Use the Traveler app's offline mode to access all your trip details without an internet connection.
